{% filter markdown %} The Articles plot shows impact metrics, `C`itation counts (when a RIS file is provided), `A`bstract match counts, `T`itle match counts, and similarity scores. - Journals are ordered by the sum of `C`, `A`, and `T`, then by the sum of similarity scores for articles returned across abstract and title [Jane](https://jane.biosemantics.org/) searches. - Article similarity scores come from the Lucene search engine used by Jane. - The metric used for the Impact bar chart be changed via dropdown. Missing values are indicated with gray bars. Hovel tooltips indicate impact values and journal tags from Jane, including indexing status in Medline and PubMed Central, and open access status. - Hovering over Cited / Title / Abstract boxes and the Similarity scatter plot shows the corresponding articles. - Markers in the Similarity scatter plot are colored green for articles found in both abstract and title searches. Clicking a marker opens the article page in PubMed. {% endfilter %}
